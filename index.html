Kombucha Creations

Recently, I have been overly interested in fermentation, more so the art of Kombucha. Now hold on a minute, this does have relevance to computer science, you just have to get some nice contextual background info. Kombucha involves fermenting very sweet tea with the presence of a SCOBY (Symbiotic Community Of Bacteria and Yeast). Whenever I get free time, I usually enjoy reading people's posts on the Kombucha subreddit flaming noobs for posting the same questions all the time, what a lovely community I must say. However, I have also been slowly developing a GUI application in Java with the JavaFX framework to benefit my kombucha brewing activity. It's been a nice learning curve, based all of my program off of OOP principles and design so I think I'm pretty well off in terms of scalability. The app is based around keeping track of every active batch of kombucha you have lying around and organizing any important information along with it using JSON and Google's annoying GSON library.
